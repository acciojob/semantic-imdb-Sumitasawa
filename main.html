Summary

Iron Man follows Tony Stark, a genius billionaire and industrialist, who is captured by terrorists and builds a high-tech armored suit to escape. Realizing the responsibility that comes with his power, he becomes the superhero Iron Man, fighting evil and protecting the world.

Actors

Robert Downey Jr. as Tony Stark / Iron Man

Also featuring: Gwyneth Paltrow as Pepper Potts, Jeff Bridges as Obadiah Stane, and Terrence Howard as James Rhodes.
